📚 What We Do

Focused Exam Preparation

I specialise in preparing students for the Matura and 8th Grade English exams. Every student is different, so every programme is tailored to their specific exam, level, and goals.

  • ✓ Exam-specific preparation — Targeted coaching for both the Matura and 8th Grade exam formats
  • ✓ Past papers & practice — Working through real exam papers to understand the format and expectations
  • ✓ Detailed feedback — Personalised feedback after every session on strengths and areas to improve
  • ✓ Homework & practice — Targeted exercises between sessions to build confidence and skills
  • ✓ Bespoke learning materials — Proprietary worksheets and resources designed specifically for exam success
  • ✓ Exam technique — Developing strategies for time management, question interpretation, and maximising marks
  • ✓ Speaking & writing skills — Building fluency and accuracy for the oral and written components

Who is this for?

  • 📘 8th Grade Exam — Students in their final year of primary school preparing for the Egzamin Ósmoklasisty
  • 📗 Matura Exam — Students in their final year of secondary school preparing for the Matura exam
